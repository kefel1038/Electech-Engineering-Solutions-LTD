Our Story

Founded on Expertise & Passion

From a vision to transform Uganda's engineering landscape, Electech was built by engineers who understand the challenges and opportunities of African infrastructure.

Electech Engineering Solutions Ltd was established by a team of experienced engineers with a clear mission: to deliver world-class engineering solutions tailored to the unique needs of East Africa. Our founder, with over a decade of experience across electrical, telecommunications, and renewable energy sectors, recognised a gap in the market for a company that combines international technical standards with deep local knowledge.

What began as a specialised electrical consultancy has grown into a multi-disciplinary engineering firm serving government, private sector, and development organisations. We have delivered projects ranging from rural solar electrification programs to complex industrial automation systems, always maintaining the same commitment to quality, safety, and innovation.

Featured Projects

Delivering impact through engineering excellence across sectors.

Electrical

Industrial Power Distribution – Luzira Factory

Challenge: A large manufacturing plant required a complete power distribution overhaul to support 40% capacity expansion.

Solution: Designed and installed 2MVA substation with redundant feeds, automatic transfer switching, and power factor correction.

Result: Zero unplanned downtime in 18 months, 15% energy savings through power factor optimisation.

Power SystemsSubstationsManufacturing
Solar

500kW Hybrid Solar – Greenfield Academy

Challenge: A large educational institution faced unreliable grid power and high electricity costs exceeding UGX 60M monthly.

Solution: Installed 500kW hybrid solar PV system with 400kWh lithium-ion battery storage and diesel generator integration.

Result: 62% reduction in grid dependency, monthly savings of UGX 37M, ROI achieved in 3.2 years.

Solar PVHybridEnergy Storage
Telecommunication

200km Fiber Backbone – Eastern Uganda

Challenge: Underserved districts in eastern Uganda lacked reliable broadband connectivity for government services and schools.

Solution: Deployed 200km of aerial and buried fiber optic cable with 10Gbps backbone capacity and 25 access points.

Result: Connected 50+ government offices and 30 schools to high-speed internet, serving 200,000+ residents.

Fiber OpticBackboneConnectivity
Automation

SCADA System – Ggaba Water Treatment

Challenge: Manual operations at a municipal water treatment plant led to inefficiencies and delayed response to system faults.

Solution: Implemented full SCADA system with PLC controls, remote sensors, and real-time monitoring dashboard.

Result: 40% reduction in operational costs, 90% faster fault response, 24/7 remote monitoring capability.

SCADAPLCIoT
ICT

Tier III Data Center – Kampala

Challenge: A financial institution needed a resilient, secure data center to support digital banking and regulatory compliance.

Solution: Designed and deployed Tier III data center with N+1 redundancy, biometric access, and 24/7 environmental monitoring.

Result: Achieved 99.98% uptime, passed regulatory audit with zero findings, supported 300% digital transaction growth.

Data CenterCloudSecurity

AI Smart Grid Prototype

Solution: Developed AI-powered smart grid prototype with IoT sensors, predictive load balancing, and automated fault isolation.
