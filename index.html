About Electech

Engineering Innovation. Powering Possibilities.

Electech Engineering Solutions Ltd was established by a team of experienced engineers with a clear mission: to deliver world-class engineering solutions tailored to the unique needs of East Africa.

Our founder, Eng. Lubega Felix Ken, recognised a gap in the market for a company that combines international engineering standards with deep local knowledge. Today, we serve government institutions, private enterprises, and development organisations across the region.

Mission

To deliver innovative, reliable, and sustainable engineering solutions that improve lives across Africa.

Vision

To become East Africa's leading engineering and technology solutions company.

Values

Innovation, Integrity, Excellence, Professionalism, Sustainability.
